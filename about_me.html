Born and raised in the Philippines, Shermaine Dayot moved to the US with her family for better opportunities, to live better lives. Now, based in Los Angeles and Orange County, she studies electrical engineering at the University of California, Irvine with the specialization in digital signal processing. Passionate about helping others, Shermaine hopes to become a positive impact on people and someday change the world using the skills she has learned and the knowledge she has acquired throughout the years.

In her spare time, Shermaine enjoys spending time with people she loves, painting, dancing, watching anime and watching food videos on Youtube. With this website she created, she would like to showcase her passions in life.
